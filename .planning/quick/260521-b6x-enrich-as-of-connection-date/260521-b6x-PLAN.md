<correct_method>
The validated as-of-connection-date method (from the quick-task background):

1. Navigate to the connection's `/in/<slug>/details/experience/` page (full experience
   history), NOT just the top experience card.
2. Read the contact's `linkedinConnectionDate` via `GET /api/contacts/<id>` (field
   `linkedinConnectionDate`).
3. Parse each experience entry's date range (start–end; "Present" = ongoing). LinkedIn
   groups multiple roles under one company header — handle grouped sub-roles.
4. Select the role whose date span CONTAINS the connection date. For a grouped company
   entry, use the matching sub-role title + the parent company name.
5. Fallbacks:
   - Connection date PREDATES earliest listed role → write null for both fields, log the
     limitation (e.g. "profile history starts <year>, predates connection date").
   - Connection date lands in an employment GAP → use closest prior role and flag it.
   - MULTIPLE roles overlap (concurrent) → pick the primary/full-time role, note concurrency.
6. Write back via `PATCH /api/contacts/<id>/enrichment` with `companyAtConnection` +
   `roleAtConnection` (null allowed). Endpoint stamps `enrichmentStatus='enriched'`.

Note: The Overview paragraph ("the company and role they held at the time of connection")
is ALREADY correct — do not change it. The contradiction is localized to the Step 4 wording
in SKILL.md and the Profile-page Step 3 wording in linkedin-navigation.md.
</correct_method>
