Correct the current-vs-at-connection contact data model surfaced after phase 10.

The LinkedIn connections-export Company/Position columns are the person's CURRENT role as of export, NOT their role at time of connection. The import route currently mis-seeds the at-connection fields from those same CSV columns and marks rows 'enriched', which permanently empties the enrichment queue. The triage card only renders one (mislabeled) line. ~1500 already-imported rows carry at-connection == current and are wrongly 'enriched'.

This plan: (1) fixes import seeding, (2) shows both lines in the triage card, (3) wires the current fields through the list, (4) ships a guarded dry-run backfill to reset the legacy rows.

Purpose: Restore the enrichment pipeline (queue must be populated) and give triage accurate now/at-connection context. Output: Corrected import route, two-line card, wired list, idempotent backfill script.
