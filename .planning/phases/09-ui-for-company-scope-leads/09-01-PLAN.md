<objective>
Make the two leaf job-lead client components render company-scope leads (`linkedinJobUrl === null`) cleanly, and pin the behavior with SSR-structural rendered tests.

- JL-C8 (detail, `scrape-results.tsx`): for a company-scope lead, promote the company name into the prominent `CardTitle` slot with a "Company scrape" badge; suppress the role-title line; keep the already-present "View Job Posting" null-guard. Job-URL leads unchanged.

Output: 2 modified components + 2 new test files. No schema, API, or RSC-query changes.

Scope locks (do not deviate): every visual/structural/copy decision is fixed by `09-UI-SPEC.md` and `09-CONTEXT.md` (D-01..D-03, CD-01..CD-04). Implement those decisions verbatim — do not re-derive icon choice, badge copy, variants, or placement. Employee-count display is DEFERRED — do NOT add it.
