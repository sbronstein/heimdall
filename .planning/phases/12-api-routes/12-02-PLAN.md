<objective>
Deliver the email-collection surface: list a campaign's emails (with the contact joined and an optional status filter), bulk-add contacts as deduped `pending` email rows, inline-edit an email, and hard-delete one. Plus the additive `GET /api/contacts` filters (howMet + connection-year range) the campaign builder uses to find the contacts it will bulk-add.

Purpose: CAMP-07 (bulk dedup insert — one outreach_email per contact, can't add twice) is the core "select recipients" capability; the contacts filters (D-07) feed it. The email list + counts support CAMP-08 progress views.
Output: Two new route files + one modified route (`contacts/route.ts`), all envelope-compliant, Zod-validated, timeline-logged.
</objective>
