Deliver the email status lifecycle and the two skill write-back routes that follow approval. The `/status` route is the only transition-guarded write in the system: it enforces `canEmailTransition()`, the CD-03 "can't approve empty" guard, and the D-05 regenerate-reset semantics. `/recipient` (discovery skill, Phase 17) and `/draft` (drafting skill, Phase 17) are simple campaign-scoped write-backs that share the same shape.

Purpose: phase criterion 4 (lifecycle transitions; invalid → 400 with reason) and the GEN-05 write-back surface (the API accepts skill write-backs; generation logic itself stays in the skill). Output: Three new PATCH route files, envelope-compliant, timeline-logged, campaign-scoped.
